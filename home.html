Meeting Analytics System

Explore structured meeting data, identify high-risk participants, and gain insights into observer behavior through interactive analysis.

Entities of the system:

  • Participant — attends meetings and reports risk of being observed
  • Meeting — event with date and location
  • Observer — monitoring agents and their preferred operating location
  • Observation — record of an observer tracking a participant during a meeting, including the method used and a confidence level representing observation quality
